Site Plan

Site Name

Cornerstone Coffee House — From our carefully roasted beans to freshly baked pastries, everything we serve is made with care and excellence. In the Bibe, Christ is called the chief cornerstone - the foundation on which faith is build. I chose this name to serve as reflection of a christian identity, a desire to build business on faith, and a reminder of spiritual grounding.

Site Purpose

The site serves as the welcoming online home for the cafe', providing menu information, building commujnity and fellowship, sharing our mission and values, and highlighting business purposes and social impact.

Scenarios

  • “What is this coffee shop about?”
  • “What is on the menu?”

Color Schema

- Orange (#e67e22): Used for buttons and highlights.
- Deep Blue (#2a6592): Used for headings and navigation bar.
- Light Gray (#fdfdfd): Background color for readability.

Typography

- Roboto: Used for body text (clean and modern).
- Montserrat: Used for headings (bold and easy to scan).
